Section 07

Sales System

A repeatable, human-led process that converts warm leads into $9,000 patients — consistently, not accidentally.

01

Lead In

Form submit or WhatsApp inquiry via LP or Instagram DM. Auto-response within 5 minutes.

02

Qualify

Coordinator assesses: hair loss stage, graft need, budget range, travel readiness. 3-question WhatsApp sequence.

03

Consult

Video call with Dr. Orn or senior coordinator. Photo assessment. Personalised case review. Trust-building session.

04

Proposal

Custom PDF quote sent within 24 hrs. Package breakdown. Transparent pricing. FAQ doc attached. No pressure.

05

Close

Follow-up WhatsApp 48–72 hrs later. Address objections. Booking deposit (20%) confirms the date. Welcome kit sent.

CRM & Follow-Up System

No lead left behind

  • All leads logged in CRM (HubSpot Free / WhatsApp CRM tool)
  • Lead status tags: New → Qualified → Consulted → Proposed → Booked → Completed → Referral
  • Automated WhatsApp reminders at days 3, 7, 14 for non-responders
  • Monthly newsletter to unconverted leads (results + offers)
  • Post-procedure: 30-day check-in → Request for testimonial/referral
Closing Strategy — Objection Handling

Every "no" has a response

  • "Is it safe?" → Dr. Orn credentials video + accreditation docs + testimonials
  • "It's too far" → Full concierge package walkthrough + "SG is 2.5 hours" framing
  • "Too expensive" → Comparison chart vs local + payment plan option
  • "Not sure about results" → 6-month photo case gallery + patient video call intro
  • "Need to think" → Value summary doc + 7-day soft deadline on current pricing
